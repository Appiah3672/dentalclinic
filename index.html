Preventive
Care

  • Routine Cleanings:
    Regular dental cleanings to remove plaque, tartar, and stains.
  • Fluoride Treatments:
    Professional fluoride applications to strengthen tooth enamel and prevent decay.
  • Dental Sealants:
    Clear coatings applied to molars to prevent bacteria and food particles from accumulating.

Cosmetic
Care

  • Teeth Whitening:
    Professional teeth whitening treatments, such as Zoom or LED whitening.
  • Veneers:
    Thin, custom-made porcelain shells bonded to front teeth to improve appearance.
  • Crowns:
    Custom-made caps to restore damaged or discoloured teeth.
  • Dental Bonding:
    Tooth-coloured resin applied to repair chipped, cracked, or gapped teeth.

Restorative
Care

  • Fillings:
    Tooth-coloured fillings to repair cavities and restore tooth function.
  • Crowns:
    Custom-made caps to restore damaged or decayed teeth.
  • Bridges:
    Fixed appliances to replace missing teeth and restore chewing.
